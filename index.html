Well-rounded, innovative, and ambitious leaders building tomorrow's world.

McMaster Makers works to build and maintain a collaborative, interdisciplinary, and inclusive Maker community through student designed and led hands-on project workshops. The aim of McMaster Makers is to empower individuals in the McMaster Community through practical STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Mathematics) education. This allows our community to develop the skills and leverage the cooperative forces necessary to turn their vision into reality.
